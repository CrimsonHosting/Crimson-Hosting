Section 02

Review Process

Before your site goes live, you'll have dedicated review rounds to request edits. Reviews included in your plan do not count toward your monthly content changes.

Ember

  • 1 final review after initial build
  • Review edits do not count toward content changes
  • After approval, further edits count toward the monthly content limit

Crimson

  • 2 review rounds included
  • Review edits do not count toward content changes
  • After approval, further edits count toward the monthly content limit

Obsidian

  • 2 review rounds included
  • Review edits do not count toward content changes
  • After approval, further edits count toward the monthly content limit

Section 03

Monthly Service Scope

Your active subscription keeps your site running and maintained. Here's what that covers and what happens if it lapses.

What's included every month

  • Website hosting and maintenance
  • Full access to your website while subscription is active
  • Ongoing updates within your plan's monthly limits

After subscription ends

  • Website access is tied to an active subscription. If canceled or unpaid, the website will be taken offline

Section 04

Update Turnaround

Once a content change is submitted, here's how quickly you can expect it to be completed.

24 – 72 hour completion window

Updates are typically completed within 24 to 72 hours, depending on plan tier and the complexity of the request. More involved changes may take longer, we'll communicate any delays proactively.
